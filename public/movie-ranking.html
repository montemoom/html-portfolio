The Best Movies According to Cathy

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Hidden Figures

This is my favorite based on true events drama movie. Very inspiring group of women.

Mr. and Mrs. Smith

Two of my all-time favorite actor and actress - Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie. Great action-comedy-love story.

Moana

One of Disney's all time favorite movie. Characteristics and qualities that I admire - bravery, perseverance and true to herself.

Hatchi

Get ready to cry. I love dog movies. This is based on a real dog in Japan and his loyalty to his master. Don't forget a tissue box.

Togo

OK, another dog movie. I just love dog movies.This is based on true events.
